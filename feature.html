Features

  • Stunning Display: The iPhone 17 Pro's "stunning" display features include exceptional 3,000 nits peak outdoor brightness, a smooth 120Hz ProMotion adaptive refresh rate, and an advanced anti-reflective coating with Ceramic Shield 2 durability.
  • Powerful Performance: Runs on iOS 26 and integrates deeply with Apple Intelligence, offering features like AI-generated "Genmoji", "Clean Up" for removing photo distractions, Live Translation, and improved Writing Tools.
  • Ample Storage: The iPhone 17 Pro offers ample built-in NVMe storage options ranging from 256GB up to 1TB but does not support expandable storage via memory cards.
  • Triple Camera System: A triple 48MP Pro Fusion camera system including a main, ultra-wide, and telephoto lens.
  • Crystal Clear Selfies: An 18MP Center Stage front camera that provides flexible framing options, smarter group selfies, and dual-capture video capabilities (simultaneous front and rear recording).
  • Advanced iOS 26: The iPhone 17 Pro comes pre-installed with iOS 26, which introduces a new design and a number of system-wide and application-specific features.
  • All-Day Battery life: The iPhone 17 Pro is designed for all-day endurance with a large 4,252 mAh battery that provides up to 33 hours of video playback and supports rapid charging capabilities.
  • Wireless Charging: The iPhone 17 Pro supports 25W wireless charging via both the MagSafe and the newer Qi2 standards, ensuring fast, magnetic alignment for efficient power delivery.
  • Sleek Design: The Apple iPhone 17 Pro features a sleek and durable design characterized by a new heat-forged aluminum unibody enclosure, a redesigned camera plateau, and a more resilient Ceramic Shield 2 front panel.
  • Vibrant Color: The standout "vibrant" shade, described as a warm, energetic tone with copper undertones that shifts between vibrant coral in natural light and deep amber indoors.
  • IP 68 Rating: The iPhone 17 Pro is rated IP68 under IEC standard 60529, providing robust protection against both dust and water ingress.
  • 5G Connectivity: The iPhone 17 Pro offers comprehensive 5G connectivity, supporting both the widely available sub-6GHz bands and the ultra-fast mmWave spectrum (in select regions), powered by an advanced modem and the Apple N1 networking chip.
  • Dual-SIM Support: The iPhone 17 Pro offers robust dual SIM support, allowing users to have two active phone lines simultaneously. The exact configuration, however, varies by region, primarily relying on eSIM technology.
  • Advanced Sensors: The phone features dual ambient light sensors that help the display accurately adjust brightness and color temperature to the surrounding environment for a better viewing experience.
