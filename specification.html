Specifications

  • Display: 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with ProMotion (120Hz) and up to 3000 nits peak outdoor brightness.
  • Processor: Apple A19 Pro chip (3nm process) with a 6-core CPU, 6-core GPU, and 16-core Neural Engine.
  • RAM: 12GB
  • Storage: 256GB, 512GB, and 1TB options.
  • Camera (Rear): Triple 48MP Fusion system: Main (f/1.6 aperture), Ultra Wide (f/2.2 aperture), and Telephoto (f/2.8 aperture) with up to 8x optical-quality zoom.
  • Camera (Front): 18MP Center Stage camera with autofocus and a square sensor for flexible framing.
  • OS: iOS 26 with integrated Apple Intelligence features.
  • Battery: Up to 31 hours of video playback (on the standard iPhone 17 Pro model).
  • Wireless Charging: The iPhone 17 Pro supports 25W wireless charging via both the MagSafe and the newer Qi2 standards, ensuring fast, magnetic alignment for efficient power delivery.
  • Wireless Charging: Yes
  • Dimensions: 50.0 mm (height) x 71.9 mm (width) x 8.75 mm (depth).
  • Color: Silver, Cosmic Orange, Blue and Deep
  • IP Rating: IP68 rating for splash, water, and dust resistance.
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6, 5G, and a second-generation Ultra Wideband chip.
  • SIM Slots: Nano-SIM + eSIM (Hybrid)
  • Sensors: A LiDAR Scanner, a second-generation Ultra Wideband chip, and an innovative 18MP Center Stage front camera with a square sensor for flexible framing,Magnetometer Array/Accessory Identification NFC.
